Fainting Couch

Appreciation Page

Why have we, as a society, forgotten about fainting couches? They have immense utility!

Oh how horrible! You're a fair maiden, overwhelmed, with nowhere to faint. We've all been there honey. Do yourself a favor and make sure that you have one of these bad boys in every room of your house! Better yet, make sure that you have one in each corner. That way, no matter where you're standing, you have somewhere to faint in a proper and dramatic manner.
